# Task 36 — Frontpage explanatory text

**Spec**: `specs/SPEC.md`

## Description

Add short, reassuring copy to the frontpage so visitors instantly understand what the app does and that their images stay private.

## Deliverables

- Add two short lines of text visible on the landing/upload screen:
  1. **What it does** — e.g. "Turn any picture into a dotted HTML page."
  2. **Privacy** — e.g. "Your image never leaves your browser. No uploads, no servers."
- Text should be concise (one sentence each, max) — people don't read.
- Style according to the existing design system (`specs/ux-styleguide.md`): muted, minimal, small type.
- Text should not compete with the upload area — it's secondary/supportive.

## Acceptance

- Two short lines of copy are visible on the frontpage before an image is loaded.
- The copy communicates: (a) what the app does and (b) that processing is 100% local.
- Text disappears or becomes unobtrusive once an image is loaded.
